About Simul-AI-tor

Simul-AI-tor is a clinical history-taking simulation tool designed to help healthcare professionals and trainees practice patient consultations. The simulated patient is powered by advanced AI and provides realistic responses. This tool is developed by William Swain for educational and training purposes.
