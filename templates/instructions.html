Simulation Instructions

Welcome to Simul-AI-tor! This simulation is designed to help you practice clinical history taking.

How it works:
- Start the simulation by selecting your level of practice and country.
- Engage in a simulated consultation with a patient.
- Use the "Hint" button if you need guidance on which questions to ask next.
- Once you have interacted sufficiently (ideally providing a differential diagnosis, a management plan, and closing the consultation), request detailed feedback.

  • Dual-Process Theory: Balance quick, intuitive judgments with more deliberate, analytical thinking to avoid common cognitive biases.

We hope this simulation helps you enhance both your history taking and clinical decision-making skills.
